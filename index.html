Technology

I have always been intigued by new technology. During my years in high school I would spend hours out of every week watching videos on the neweset phones and laptops hitting the market. I have seen the astonishing rate of innovation in the technology field and I want to be a part in it. I have been an apple fanboy since I saw the first Iphone debut in 2007 but was unable to afford and Iphone of my own until the 4s was released. After a few years of using IOS and MacOS I have begun to see the increasing competition and innovation from Apples competitors. While many people love the familiarity of the IOS software that sparingly changes with time, there is a platform that provides more control and more features. This platform is Android and while it may have been unattractive and buggy at its first release, it has came about with many great changes to make this software user-friendly.
